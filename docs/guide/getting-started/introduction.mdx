# Introduction

The **KoAkademy Portal** is your central hub for all academic activities. From checking your class schedule to viewing your grades and managing tuition payments, everything is just a few clicks away.

This portal is designed to make your student life easier by providing quick access to important information and tools you need for your studies.

For developers and maintainers, this page describes the expected user-facing behavior that frontend and backend changes should preserve.

## Key Features

- **Academics**: View enrolled subjects, weekly schedules, and grades.
- **Finances**: Track tuition fees, view payment history, and download Statements of Account (SOA).
- **Profile**: Manage your personal information and security settings.
- **Support**: Access FAQs and contact IT support directly.

## Getting Started

1.  **Log In**: Use your student email and password.
2.  **Explore**: Check the dashboard for an overview of your status.
3.  **Navigate**: Use the sidebar to access specific features.
