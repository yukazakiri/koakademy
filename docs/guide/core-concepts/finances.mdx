# Finances

Stay on top of your financial obligations.

For developers, this page describes the expected finance-related surfaces that should remain stable when billing or payment workflows change.

## Tuition & Fees

View a detailed breakdown of your assessment for the current semester, including:

- Tuition Fees
- Laboratory Fees
- Miscellaneous Fees

## Payment History

See a record of all payments made to your account, including dates, reference numbers, and amounts.

## Statement of Account (SOA)

Need an official copy of your balance? You can generate and download a PDF version of your Statement of Account directly from the portal.
